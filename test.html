Rush Hour 3

Movie Synopsis

*Rush Hour 3* is a high-octane action-comedy that reunites the dynamic duo, Detective Carter (Chris Tucker) and Chief Inspector Lee (Jackie Chan). This time, their thrilling adventure takes them to Paris, where they must unravel a conspiracy involving the Chinese Triad. Packed with comedic moments, stunning martial arts, and exciting chase scenes, *Rush Hour 3* delivers non-stop entertainment.
